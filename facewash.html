Types of Face Washes For Different Skin Types

For a layman, a face wash is just as simple as a gel/cream that helps in washing the face but those who have been buying face wash as per their skin type for a long time knows there are different face washes available for different purposes. Let’s take a quick look.

Face Wash for Oily Skin

If you are someone who has a lot of sebum secretion and finds your skin sticky and oily, you probably have oily skin. Hence, you need a face wash that suits your oily skin. Face wash made for oily skin has ingredients that can remove excess oil from your face and leave your skin with the required moisture to breathe and stay healthy. Most face wash for oily skin comes in a gel sort of texture, so pick the right face wash wisely!.

Face Wash for Dry Skin

People with dry skin always dread that using face wash will make their skin drier. But it’s absolutely wrong because if you use a face wash that suits other skin types and not dry skin, it will give you results accordingly. Therefore, it’s important to choose the right face wash for yourself. Face wash for dry skin is exactly made with elements that will leave your skin moist, nourished and will not let your skin get drier with its thick and moisturising formula.

Face Wash for Combination Skin

Combination skin is all about having a combo of oily and dry skin, which needs a special kind of face wash where ingredients won’t take away excess oil from the dry area and leave extra moisture on the oily area. So if you have combination skin, you need to find the right face wash accordingly.
